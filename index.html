Our Story

Crafting Journeys Since 2015

Established in 2015 in the enchanting city of Tripoli, Al Koura, near Haykaliyeh Hospital Noujoum Building, our journey began with a vision to redefine travel. Founded by the dynamic duo of Mr. Ahmad Mahfouz and Mr. Saad Hamdi, Black Pearl Tours swiftly emerged as a trusted name in the travel industry, driven by a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating lasting memories.
